What We Do Clubs Brands Players Fan Match

  • 9ineSports is the Ultimate Sports Network. It is the first complete, all in one solution for Sports Fans to connect with their favorite clubs, players, brands, and also each other.

    We revolutionize the way people follow, share, and live their Sports Passion.

    Through the implementation of our web and mobile platforms we focus on establishing tangible growth in the Engagement and Monetization of Sports Fans while providing the most inclusive Network for them to interact.

  • For our official partnered clubs we offer a personalized profile on our network as well as a personalized shop where fans can purchase gear, tickets, and tours of the stadium.

    We expand fan outreach by connecting our growing network of users to all of the news surrounding your club.

    We've already done the work for you and we offer a great opportunity to expand your existing social network to our global platform.

  • We know brands are an important part of the Sports World. We want to help expand brand outreach and put their best products in our fans hands.

    Brands have the opportunity to partner with us and have an exlusive spot to sell products to our community of fans on the 9ineSports Shop.

    Not only can we increase brand sales, but we also increase their social network performance by connecting their media activity to thousands of new fans.

  • For players we offer a dedicated group of fans to connect with, thus increasing their popularity. With increase popularity comes more sponsorships.

    Not only do we connect our fans to their idols social activity on other networks such as Facebook and Twitter, but we also customize a personal page and profile for each player.

  • Fan Match is a web application and game that allows fans to play games against each other in an effort to Engage their support for their favorite teams.

    The app recreates real matches so users may support their favorite teams with their votes and participation against other fans.

    Victory depends entirely on the votes of the users and their friends, not the actual outcome of the game.

    Users are rewarded with points for each vote, for sharing with their friends, and for winning the Fan Match. These accumulated points can be exchanged for digital prizes on our platform.
